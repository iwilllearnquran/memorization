📖 Surah: Al-Baqarah(البقرة) : Ayah 98

مَن Whoever مَن cond كَانَ is كَانَ verb عَدُوًّا an enemy عَدُوّ noun لِّلَّهِ (to) Allah لِّ ٱللَّه prep+pn وَمَلَٰٓئِكَتِهِۦ and His Angels وَ مَلَك هِۦ conj+noun+pron وَرُسُلِهِۦ and His Messengers وَ رَسُول هِۦ conj+noun+pron وَجِبْرِيلَ and Jibreel وَ جِبْرِيل conj+pn وَمِيكَىٰلَ and Meekael وَ مِيكَىٰل conj+pn فَإِنَّ then indeed فَ إِنّ rslt+acc اللَّهَ Allah ٱللَّه pn عَدُوٌّ (is) an enemy عَدُوّ noun لِّلْكَٰفِرِينَ to the disbelievers لِّ لْ كَٰفِرُون prep+det+noun
Nominative (Rafʿ / Subject)
Accusative (Naṣb / Object)
Genitive (Jarr / Possession)
Verb (Fiʿl / Action)

🌍 English Translation

Whoever is an enemy to Allah and His angels and His messengers and Gabriel and Michael - then indeed, Allah is an enemy to the disbelievers.
