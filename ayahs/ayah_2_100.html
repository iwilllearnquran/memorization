📖 Surah: Al-Baqarah(البقرة) : Ayah 100

أَوَكُلَّمَا And is (it not that) whenever أَ وَ كُلَّمَا intg+sup+t عَٰهَدُوا۟ they took عَٰهَدَ وا۟ verb+pron عَهْدًا a covenant عَهْد noun نَّبَذَهُۥ threw it away نَبَذَ هُۥ verb+pron فَرِيقٌ a party فَرِيق noun مِّنْهُم of them مِن هُم prep+pron بَلْ Nay بَل ret أَكْثَرُهُمْ most of them أَكْثَر هُمْ noun+pron لَا (do) not لَا neg يُؤْمِنُونَ believe ءَامَنَ ونَ verb+pron
Nominative (Rafʿ / Subject)
Accusative (Naṣb / Object)
Genitive (Jarr / Possession)
Verb (Fiʿl / Action)

🌍 English Translation

Is it not [true] that every time they took a covenant a party of them threw it away? But, [in fact], most of them do not believe.
