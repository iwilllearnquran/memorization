📖 Surah: Al-Baqarah(البقرة) : Ayah 18

صُمٌّۢ Deaf أَصَمّ noun بُكْمٌ dumb أَبْكَم noun عُمْىٌ blind أَعْمَىٰ noun فَهُمْ so they فَ هُمْ conj+pron لَا not لَا neg يَرْجِعُونَ [they] will not return رَجَعَ ونَ verb+pron
Nominative (Rafʿ / Subject)
Accusative (Naṣb / Object)
Genitive (Jarr / Possession)
Verb (Fiʿl / Action)

🌍 English Translation

Deaf, dumb and blind - so they will not return [to the right path].
