📖 Surah: Hud(هود) : Ayah 1

الٓر Alif Lam Ra الٓر Initial Part كِتَٰبٌ (This is) a Book كِتَٰب Noun أُحْكِمَتْ are perfected أُحْكِمَتْ Verb ءَايَٰتُهُۥ its Verses ءَايَة هُۥ Noun+Pron. ثُمَّ moreover ثُمّ Conj. فُصِّلَتْ explained in detail فَصَّلَ Verb مِن from (he One Who) مِن Preposition لَّدُنْ from (he One Who) لَّدُن Time Adverb حَكِيمٍ (is) All-Wise حَكِيم Noun خَبِيرٍ All-Aware خَبِير Adj
Nominative (Rafʿ / Subject)
Accusative (Naṣb / Object)
Genitive (Jarr / Possession)

🌍 English Translation

Alif, Lam, Ra. [This is] a Book whose verses are perfected and then presented in detail from [one who is] Wise and Acquainted.
