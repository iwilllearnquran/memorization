📖 Surah: Al-Baqarah(البقرة) : Ayah 81

بَلَىٰ Yes بَلَىٰ Answer Part مَن whoever مَن Conditional Part كَسَبَ earned كَسَبَ Verb سَيِّئَةً evil سَيِّئَة Noun وَأَحَٰطَتْ and surrounded him وَ أَحَاطَ Conj.+Verb بِهِۦ with بِ هِۦ Preposition+Pron. خَطِيٓـَٔتُهُۥ his sins خَطِيَٓٔة هُۥ Noun+Pron. فَأُو۟لَٰٓئِكَ [so] those فَ أُولَٰٓئِك Result Part+Demonstrative أَصْحَٰبُ (are the) companions أَصْحَٰب Noun النَّارِ (of) the Fire ٱل نَار Al++Noun هُمْ they هُمْ Pron. فِيهَا in it فِى هَا Preposition+Pron. خَٰلِدُونَ (will) abide forever خَٰلِد Noun

🌍 English Translation

Yes, whoever earns evil and his sin has encompassed him - those are the companions of the Fire; they will abide therein eternally.
